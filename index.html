What is a Carbon Footprint?

Your carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases, especially carbon dioxide (CO₂), released into the atmosphere as a result of your everyday actions — from driving a car and charging your phone, to the food you eat and clothes you buy.

Why It Matters

The more CO₂ we emit, the more we contribute to global warming and climate change. Understanding your carbon footprint is the first step to reducing it and creating a more sustainable future.
